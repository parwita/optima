Rusty Nails and Tetanus

Understanding the bacterial infection by Clostridium Tetani. This disease can be triggered by both minor and severe wounds

Tetanus is a bacterial infection caused by Clostridium tetani. In Bali, there is a risk of getting infected, especially if you have a dirty wound or get injured by a rusty object. If you sustain an injury, it’s crucial to promptly clean the wound area and cover it with a bandage to prevent bacterial spores from entering. Equally important is getting a tetanus vaccination.

The treatment we offer includes:

  • Wound dressing / modern wound dressing
  • Bandage / waterproof bandage
  • Wound exploration with local anaesthetic
  • Antibiotic (if necessary)
  • Painkiller medication
  • Tetanus vaccination

All therapies are administered based on a thorough doctor's examination, complemented by a comprehensive medical report and other necessary documents for potential insurance reimbursement.

At our center, we provide comprehensive care for tetanus, including wound management and tetanus vaccinations.
